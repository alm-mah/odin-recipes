Kraft-Dinner (KD) Recipe

This recipe originates from the side of the box the KD came in, perfected by me over years of making this stuff. This recipes will allow you to create creamy, rich and gooey macaroni and cheese (almost) staright out of the box. This recipe has been enjoyed by many, such as my sisters, my mom and me. I have received many compliments on my ailtiy to make KD and so I am glad that my recipe is finally unveiled to the world.

Ingrediants

    one to two boxes of Kraft Dinner macaroni and cheese.
    Powdered cheese packet (comes in the box)
    one to two tablespoons of butter or margarine.
    One quarter cup or on third cup of milk.
    Shredded cheese of choice. Cheedar and mozzarella recommended
    Salt and Pepper
    Olive oil (optional but reccomended)

Steps to Prepare

    Get a pot and fill it 3/4 full with water.
    Bring water to a boil, then add pasta and cook for 8-12 minutes
    Once pasta is cooked,drain water
